About Us

Natalie was 6 year old when I realised I am going round in circles trying to teach her how to be a good person. It all starts at home and the earlier you start the better. Easier said than done.

I started from making a wall chart with her good and bad deeds listed with smileys and sad face icons. We have noticed straight away with my wife a positive change in our child's behaviour and an idea of a website helping us to keep track of it all dawned on me.

That's how B.Nice started - out of a simple need to bring more happiness to our family.
